Volunteer

Volunteering with Paisley YMCA is a great way to share your skills, develop new ones and create new contacts and opportunities. There are many benefits to volunteering: knowing that you're making a contribution to your local community, enhancing your CV and job prospects. Many of our paid youth work staff started off as volunteers and you could get involved in a wide range of activities, from delivering school programmes and community projects to setting up your own social enterprise. The range of activities you could be involved in includes: Indoor/outdoor Sport activities Fundraising and publicity Administration and finance Social media and marketing Youth Mentoring Professional Development Award in Youth Work Day camps/holiday schemes and trips abroad
