Volunteer

Volunteering with Paisley YMCA is a great way to share your skills, develop new ones and create new contacts and opportunities. There are many benefits to volunteering: knowing that you're making a contribution to your local community, enhancing your CV and job prospects. Many of our paid youth work staff started off as volunteers and you could get involved in a wide range of activities, from delivering school programmes and community projects to setting up your own social enterprise. The range of activities you could be involved in includes:

  • Indoor/outdoor Sport activities
  • Fundraising and publicity
  • Administration and finance
  • Social media and marketing
  • Youth Mentoring
  • Professional Development Award in Youth Work
  • Day camps/holiday schemes and trips abroad

To find out about existing volunteering opportunities, or just to chat to someone about what you might be interested in, contact Paisley YMCA on 0141 889 2876 or email darran@paisleyymca.org
