Donate

Your support is vital to our work in helping provide facilities and activities, giving young people a future and a hope. Any contribution is greatly appreciated to support this work. JUSTGIVING.COM To send us a donation by mobile phone, please text YMCA39 followed by the amount you'd like to donate (£1, £2, £3, £4, £5 or £10) to 70070. For example, to donate £3, you would text the following to 70070: YMCA39 £3. All the money you donate goes to us, and your network provider does not charge you for the SMS you send to make the donation. You can donate up to £30 on any one day.
